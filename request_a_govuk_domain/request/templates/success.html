What happens next

  1. We will contact you within 5 working days to confirm approval, reject the application or to ask for more information.
  2. Once approved, a .gov.uk domain must be registered with the registry operator (Nominet). We will send you a unique code, which you must use to complete this registration.
If you have any questions you can email approvals@domains.gov.uk.

How appeals work

If the Domains Team rejects an application you can appeal on behalf of your registrant by emailing appeals@domains.gov.uk.

We will forward this appeal with any additional information we request or your registrant wants to provide to the Naming and Approvals Committee (NAC) and keep you up-to-date with progress.

The NAC will make a final decision and will give its reasoning.
